About

    I am currently a Senior Researcher at Microsoft M365 Research Group. Before joining Microsoft, I received my Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Dan Pei in the Netman Group. My research spans Software Engineering, Systems, and Artificial Intelligence. My research interests are primarily focused on AI for IT Operations (AIOps) / Cloud Intelligence.
    I am enthusiastic about making contributions to the AIOps ecosystem to facilitate collaboration between industry and academia. Together with Prof. Dan Pei, we launched the 4th CCF AIOps Challenge and Workshop from November 2020 to May 2021. Additionally, I served as a PC member and speaker at the 6th CCF AIOps Challenge and Workshop. Our team has made available open-source AIOps benchmarks for multiple AIOps scenarios as well as an AIOps benchmark for large language models.
    I always seek self-motivated undergraduate, master's, and Ph.D. intern students with strong programming skills. Please email me your CV if you are interested in working with me.
